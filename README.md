## What is loomkin?  

Loomkin lets AI agents work together like people do. You can create helpers fast, watch them share what they learn, and see their progress live on your screen. It remembers everything for the next time you use it. This app shows you how groups of AI can team up in real-time.

After installation, launch loomkin by clicking its icon.

- **New Mission**: Click this button to start a new AI team task.

- **History Tab**: Check previous missions to see what agents worked on.

## Troubleshooting  

Here are common issues and how to fix them:  

- **App won’t start**: Restart your PC. Make sure Windows is updated.  
- **Installation fails**: Run the installer as an administrator (right-click file, select "Run as administrator").  
- **Agents don’t appear**: Check your internet connection and restart loomkin.  
- **UI is slow or laggy**: Close other heavy apps and try again.  

If problems continue, visit the "Issues" tab on the GitHub page for community help.
